üåô Crescent Moon

A crescent moon shown in yellow on most platforms.

Facebook Messenger uses this emoji to activate dark mode. As of March 2019, send this emoji to any friend via Messenger and the dark mode setting will be enabled.

Crescent Moon was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
